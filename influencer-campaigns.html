The protected process

Permission first. Disclosure always.

01 · Rights check. Confirm the party authorizing the sample and the exact music segment.

02 · Written brief. Define deliverables, payment, timing, usage and approval boundaries.

03 · Creator match. Select for audience fit and authentic creative style—not follower count alone.

04 · Visible disclosure. Paid or gifted promotions must be clearly disclosed in the content itself.

05 · Real reporting. Report delivered posts and available platform metrics; never manufacture streams or engagement.

Transparency standard: Membership or campaign payment does not guarantee views, streams, sales, playlist placement, virality or positive coverage. Creators must follow applicable advertising-disclosure rules and each platform’s branded-content and music policies. Vietnam Combat Music does not sell artificial streams or undisclosed editorial coverage.
